# Larissa Tiles

A tile puzzle game. Cover a rectangular grid with 1×2, 2×2, and 2×3 tiles as fast as you can.

## How to play

- Choose a preset grid size (11×16, 12×15, or 13×15) or enter your own
- Select a tile from the palette and click the grid to place it
- Drag a placed tile to move it, or drag it off the grid to remove it
- The timer starts on your first placement and stops when the grid is complete
- Use ⚡ Check lines to verify no straight line passes cleanly between tiles

## Settings

Click the gear icon to customize tile groups, dimensions, colors, and limits.
