About

Larissa has 3 shapes of tiles and needs your help covering a rectangular grid. How fast can you complete the puzzle?

How to play
1Choose a preset grid (11×16, 12×15, or 13×15) or enter a custom size and tap New grid.
2Select a tile shape from the palette. Each group shows how many tiles remain.
3Click an empty cell to place a tile. A green preview means it fits; red means it doesn't.
4Drag a placed tile to move it. Drag it off the grid (or press Del) to remove it.
5Fill every cell. The timer starts on your first placement and stops when the grid is complete.
6Tap ⚡ Check lines to see if any straight line passes cleanly between tiles — a perfect tiling has none.
Tips
Use ↩ Undo to remove the last tile placed.
✕ Clear resets the board and the timer.
Customize tile groups, colors, and limits in Settings.
